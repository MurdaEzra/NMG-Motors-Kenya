About NMG Motors Kenya

Discover our story, our values, and our commitment to providing exceptional automotive experiences in Kenya.

Our Story

Founded in 2010, NMG Motors Kenya has grown to become one of the premier destinations for luxury and premium vehicles in East Africa. What began as a small dealership with a handful of vehicles has blossomed into a trusted name in the automotive industry.

Our journey has been driven by a passion for excellence and a commitment to delivering unparalleled service to our clients. Over the years, we've built strong relationships with leading manufacturers and developed a reputation for integrity and quality.
